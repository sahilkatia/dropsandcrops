ABOUT US

Drops and Crops is a website used to showcase projects that involve partnerships between the water for Food Institute and partnering Institutions. The website is managed by the Water for Food Institute. Many of the projects showcased on Drops and Crops have an emphasis on Social Innovation andstudent involvement. The website was designed and developed by interns from the University of Nebraska.
